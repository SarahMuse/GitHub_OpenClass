Summarizing the data

Can get very easily important information:

  • Minimum and maximum value of the data
  • Median and mean of the data
  • First and third quartile
summary(dat)
##        y        
##  Min.   :1.904  
##  1st Qu.:2.241  
##  Median :2.414  
##  Mean   :2.419  
##  3rd Qu.:2.568  
##  Max.   :2.984

Quartile

Quartile means one quarter of the data

  • First quartile = value of the data below which lie the 25% of the data = 25th percentile
  • Second quartile = median = 50% of the data are below the median = 50th percentile = interquartile
  • Third quartile = value of the data below which lie the 25% of the data = value of the data above which lie 25% of the data = 75th percentile

Boxplot (1)

Can summarize all those information in a picture

  • Bold horizontal bar = median
  • Whiskers show maximum and minimum values
  • Top and bottom of the box show the 75th and 25th percentile, respectively
  • Whole box shows where the middle of the data lie

boxplot(dat)
